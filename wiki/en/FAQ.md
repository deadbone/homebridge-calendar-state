# FAQ

## Does this read Google Calendar, iCloud, or CalDAV?

No. This plugin is local-rule based and intentionally avoids unnecessary network calls.

## How is this different from calendar scheduler plugins?

Calendar scheduler plugins usually watch iCal or CalDAV events and trigger sensors from event names. This plugin models recurring day-state rules such as working day, day off, WFH, office day, weekdays, and special local dates.

## Is it Verified by Homebridge?

Not yet.
